## 🚀 Summary
Obsidian for Windows is an all-in-one home for your notes that treats plain text as a feature, not a limitation. The obsidian note taking app stores everything as Markdown files on your PC, then connects them with links, tags, and a visual graph so scattered ideas become a coherent knowledge base. Because your files stay local, the experience is fast and private, and a rich ecosystem of obsidian plugins lets the app flex around your workflow. It works equally well as a quick capture tool, a study companion, or a long-term research archive for students, writers, and knowledge workers.

## 🌟 Feature Set

1. **Plain-text Markdown** 📄
   Every note is a portable obsidian md file on your own disk, so you keep full ownership and can move your vault anywhere.

2. **Linked knowledge** 🔗
   Connect notes with internal links and backlinks to turn a collection of obsidian notes into a navigable web of ideas.

3. **Visual graph** 🕸️
   Explore your vault as a network and discover relationships between topics you might otherwise miss.

4. **Plugin ecosystem** 🧩
   Extend the app with obsidian plugins for tasks, diagrams, spaced repetition, and countless other workflows.

5. **Instant search** 🔎
   Locate any note in seconds with quick switching, tags, and full-text search across your entire vault.

## ❓ FAQ
**Does Obsidian cost money?**
Obsidian is free for personal note-taking, and your files always belong to you. Optional paid services are offered separately if you decide to use them.

**Which Windows versions are supported?**
64-bit Windows 10 and Windows 11. Keeping your system updated helps everything run smoothly.

**Obsidian vs Notion — which fits me better?**
It comes down to where your notes live. Obsidian keeps them as local Markdown files you own and can use offline, while Notion is cloud-first. If local ownership, offline speed, and plugin customization are priorities, Obsidian is designed around them.

**Is my data kept private?**
Yes. Your obsidian notes are stored as plain files on your own Windows PC, so nothing has to leave your machine for the app to work.
